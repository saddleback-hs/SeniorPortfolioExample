About Me

The Artist
Behind the Work

Music inspires and informs my artwork and designs. Picking a group of songs for a project is as important as any other part of my design/creative process.

Album artwork and watercolor paintings are my two favorite artforms to observe. John Singer Sargent is my favorite watercolorist and Metallica, Baroness, Mastodon, and Deftones have created my favorite album artwork.
